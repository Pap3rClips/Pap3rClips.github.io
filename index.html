Def Con

"One of the largest hacking conferences in the world, held annually in Las Vegas. DEF CON brings together hackers, security researchers, developers, and cybersecurity professionals to exchange the latest discoveries, hacking techniques, and security solutions. It also features competitions like 'Capture The Flag' (CTF), which is among the most prestigious globally."

  • Learn More

Black Hat USA

This conference also takes place in Las Vegas just before DEF CON. Black Hat focuses more on business and professional training, featuring high-level technical sessions on emerging security threats and defense methods. It is known for its intensive workshops where participants can gain practical skills in cybersecurity.

  • Learn More

RSA Conference

Primarily held in the United States (San Francisco), the RSA Conference is one of the largest cybersecurity events in the world. While it is more oriented towards general security than specifically hacking, it attracts experts from various backgrounds to discuss cybersecurity trends, software and hardware solutions, as well as innovations in the field.
